Where You'll Stay

Accommodation Options

Private rooms for all 8 guests. Two outstanding properties — both within minutes of the beach and the heart of Noosa. Select one, or mix and match based on individual preferences.

Option A
Resort · Noosaville

RACV Noosa Resort

171 beautifully appointed rooms set across a tranquil resort complex. Features a day spa with six treatment rooms, two heated pools, water slides, a restaurant and sunset bar with wood-fired pizza, flood-lit tennis and bike hire. Private balconies and terraces throughout — a true resort escape.

🏊 Two heated pools 🧖 Day spa 🎾 Tennis court 🍕 Wood-fired restaurant
Option B
Boutique Resort · Noosa Heads

Peppers Noosa Resort & Villas

Poised on the hillside with striking views over Laguna Bay, Peppers sits nestled in greenery in the heart of Noosa. Choose from spacious, exquisitely designed apartments with premium fittings — a calmer, more intimate alternative with direct proximity to Hastings Street and the beach.

🌊 Laguna Bay views 🌿 Surrounded by nature 🏖️ Steps to Hastings St 🛁 Luxury appointments
